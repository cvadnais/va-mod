MOD Weekly

A brief video summarizing the week’s biggest news items related to Modernization or other VA objectives.

Current Episode

VA announces it’s on track to eliminate the hepatitis C virus in all Veterans willing and able to be treated, VA providers will offer Veterans a newly-approved nasal spray for treatment-resistant depression, and VA joins forces with DOD once again — this time to host a conference to address suicide in the military and Veteran communities. Captions available in English and Spanish.



Past Episodes

March 15, 2019

The president's budget request includes more funds for VA's support of Veterans; Secretary Wilkie comments on the proposed budget; and VHA is going smoke-free this year. Note: We now offer Spanish language captions.

March 8, 2019

Secretary Wilkie responds to a presidential action supporting the transition of Veterans into the Merchant Marine; by executive order, President Trump establishes the PREVENTS initiative; and 18 facilities are selected to start a "high reliability" journey.

March 1, 2019

Priority for Purple Heart recipients, Sec Wilkie on the Hill, and Congressional support for Modernization.

February 22, 2019

MODWeekly is brought to you by the VA's Office of Modernization. That's the office charged with helping VA transform into a modern, high-performing organization that simplifies operations and improves delivery of services to Veterans.
